WHAT IS ORIGAMI ?

Origami is the art of paper-folding.
Its name derives from Japanese words ori (“folding”) and kami (“paper”).
Traditional origami consists of folding a single sheet of square paper into a sculpture without cutting, gluing, taping, or even marking it.
